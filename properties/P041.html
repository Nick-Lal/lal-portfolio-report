Field research (July 2026)

Geocoder check: The geocoder cannot geocode "E-1 Downingtown Commons" at all (zero results), so no imagery classification was possible.

Web search: The geocoder query for "E-1 Downingtown Commons, Pennsylvania" returned zero results (empty JSON array), the geocoder cannot resolve this address at all, so no imagery was pulled per the task's conditional instructions. Web search (with and without municipality) surfaced a real, currently-existing complex called "Downingtown Commons": a 64-unit condo community at 335 E Lancaster Ave, Downingtown, PA 19335, built ~1965-1995, with unit designations like A21, D-22, F23, A13 (letter-number style consistent with "E-1"). Sources: Apartments.com, Homes.com, LivingPlaces.com, TheCyrTeam, Zumper, Craigslist listings. However, cross-checking the Chester County ArcGIS parcel layer (authoritative source) shows NO parcel at 335 E Lancaster Ave, NO address containing "COMMONS" anywhere in Downingtown township (MUNI=11), and NO legal description referencing "Downingtown Commons." A full pull of all ~123 parcels on E Lancaster Ave in Downingtown shows house numbers jumping from 355 down through the 340s/330s/320s with no 335 present, and no unit-lettered condo parcels at all. A separate owner-name sweep for LAL in Downingtown township returns only two already-known family parcels (315 Bradford Ave and 511 Grant Ave, both LAL AMRIT), neither connected to any Commons address.
