Field research (July 2026)

Geocoder check: The geocoder resolves "2516 Pondview Drive" to an exact-house-number match, but in Worcester Township, Montgomery County PA (ZIP 19446) -- roughly 15+ miles from East Goshen and an entirely different county, so it is not a plausible match for this property. Chester County's own parcel address field has no "Pond View," "Pondview," or "Ponds View" street in East Goshen or ZIP 19382 at all; the West Chester 19382 condo complex that real-estate sites market as "Pond View Dr" is officially addressed by the county as "Leeds Ct" / "Bidefore Ct," and no unit numbered 2516 exists there or on any true Pond View street countywide. This is a dead end: no Chester County parcel corresponds to this address under any spelling or numbering variant checked.

Web search: A web search for "2516 Pondview Drive" (with and without "East Goshen") returned no listing, rental ad, or record matching that exact address anywhere. It did surface a genuine, actively-listed street called "Pond View Dr" in West Chester, PA 19382 (Zillow, Redfin, Trulia, Apartments.com, Coldwell Banker, Movoto, Hotpads, highrises.com), part of the "Goshen Valley III" condo community (West Chester Area School District), with house numbers in the 2300s-2500s (e.g. 2305, 2320, 2408, 2414, 2503, 2506, 2509 Pond View Dr) -- a numbering pattern close to "2516." Cross-checked directly against the Chester County ArcGIS parcel layer (Parcels_owners/FeatureServer/0): that same condo complex is recorded there in East Goshen Township (MUNI 67) under the official street names "Leeds Ct" and "Bidefore Ct," numbered 2301-2504, NOT "Pond View Dr" -- the aggregator sites appear to use a marketing/complex name the county does not use for addressing. No unit numbered 2516 exists on Leeds Ct, Bidefore Ct, or any other street in that development. Exhaustive ArcGIS queries for any parcel matching "POND VIEW", "PONDVIEW", or "PONDS VIEW" anywhere in Chester County found only: (1) Pond View Dr in Uwchlan Twp (ZIP 19333) and East Whiteland Twp (ZIP 19317), house numbers capped at 363; (2) Pond View La/Way in other townships (ZIP 19355, 19460, 19335), numbers up to 953; (3) Ponds View Dr in Elk Township (ZIP 19363), numbers 101-165. None are in East Goshen, none are in ZIP 19382, and none reach house number 2516.
