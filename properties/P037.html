Field research (July 2026)

Geocoder check: The geocoder geocodes "332 Fleetwood Street, Pennsylvania" to a specific point in Coatesville, Chester County (39.9856338, -75.8203743) and third-party aggregators (UnMask, Xome) echo the same address string, but a direct query of Chester County's authoritative parcel layer shows the real Fleetwood St address range skips from the 250s straight to 299/300/301/400+ with no 332 in the county's own data - the parcel does not exist in the county record despite the geocoder producing coordinates for it. Aerial imagery could not be retrieved to visually inspect the site (ArcGIS export service returned a persistent server error).

Web search: A web search for "332 Fleetwood Street" Coatesville PA returned a people-search aggregator (UnMask, for a "Victor Noland") listing this as an address history entry, and a real-estate aggregator (Xome) listing "332 Fleetwood St, Coatesville, PA 19320" among Fleetwood St properties. Neither is a primary/county source and neither was opened for further detail (per read-only, no-account-creation rules, aggregator gateway pages were not penetrated further). Separately, a direct query of the authoritative Chester County parcel layer (Parcels_owners FeatureServer) for all LOC_ADDRESS values containing 'FLEETWOOD' returned 34 parcels, all on FLEETWOOD ST in municipality code 16 (Coatesville City), with house numbers spanning 206-475 but no 300s number below 400 except 301 (PennDOT ROW parcel) - i.e., the sequence runs ...252, 254, 256, 258, [gap], 299, 300, 301, 400... with no 332 anywhere in the county's own address field. No Lal-linked, ATL-linked, or RST-linked owner name appears on Fleetwood Street at all.

About this site. Every fact here comes from a public government record: county assessment and deed data, the PA Department of State, county Tax Claim lookups, and the PA court portal, each captured as a saved evidence artifact (268 entries). Market values and rents are model estimates, not appraisals. Data as of 2026-07-07; ownership is re-verified against the county weekly. Where a fact could not be verified it is marked Unverified rather than guessed.

Privacy. Court information appears only as aggregates: no tenant is named anywhere on this site, by design. Owners and deed parties are named exactly as they appear in public county records.
