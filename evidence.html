The evidence, linked

Every dossier on this site lists its sources. This page goes one step further: for each kind of claim the site makes, here is a piece of evidence you can open yourself, right now, and what it proves. Most links below are live government queries; they return the current record, so you are not trusting a screenshot. The working file behind this site also holds 268+ captured artifacts (screenshots, CSV exports, PDFs), available to the family and its professionals on request.

The state publishes its business registry as data. Compare two live queries:

Registry query for "ATL ASSOCIATES…" ↗ returns only an unrelated Philadelphia corporation (different officers, different address), and nothing connected to this portfolio. The same check comes up empty for RST Partners and Nagpaul Associates.

Proof of the assessed-versus-market gap

Example: 338 Joy Lane is assessed at $150,000 (county record ↗) while Redfin's July 2026 model estimate is $684,021 (captured 2026-07-06 in the working file). The gap is normal: Chester County assessments sit on an old base year, and Pennsylvania itself publishes the correction factor, currently 3.27 for this county (PA Dept. of Revenue CLR factors ↗).

What it proves: why this site never treats assessed value as market value, and where the derived ranges for unpriced parcels come from.

Proof that the taxes are clean

Tax delinquency lives in the county Tax Claim system, checked here parcel by parcel on 2026-07-06 via the county's online lookup (linked from chesco.org ↗), with the method validated against a control parcel carrying a known balance before any clean result was recorded.

What it proves: all 110 family-linked parcels showed no delinquent claim. The lookup is interactive, so each dossier records its check date and result in the field notes rather than a deep link.

About this site. Every fact here comes from a public government record: county assessment and deed data, the PA Department of State, county Tax Claim lookups, and the PA court portal, each captured as a saved evidence artifact (268 entries). Market values and rents are model estimates, not appraisals. Data as of 2026-07-07; ownership is re-verified against the county weekly. Where a fact could not be verified it is marked Unverified rather than guessed.

Privacy. Court information appears only as aggregates: no tenant is named anywhere on this site, by design. Owners and deed parties are named exactly as they appear in public county records.
